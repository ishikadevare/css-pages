About Us


Disha Motor Training School that have established in 2002 as a benchmark in the field of education are very much concerned about the increase in number of road accidents. The victims are mostly students and youngsters. The result of their concern is the start of a Disha Motor Training School which aims at imparting experience in safe driving as well as trouble shooting techniques in Motorcycles & LMVs. Our business has grown since then & Now we have many experienced driving instructors. We provide very comfortable & quality driving tuition for our customers not just to get their driving license but safe driving too for life. We also do the R.T.O related work like to obtain driving license for Two Wheelers, Three Wheelers & Four Wheelers along with insurance work for their various vehicles. We have facility of online driving Test for taking the appointment for driving Test.
